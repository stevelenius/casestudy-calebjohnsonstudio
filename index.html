Life is unexpected, complex, and beautiful. Good architecture has always expressed this in its own quiet way.

It is this search for a genuine expression of life that keeps our days interesting.

Caleb Johnson Studio is focused on rethinking the way architecturally-designed building projects are created and delivered in our region. We design, we build, and we partner with our clients to fulfill their vision.

We are an award-winning Maine architecture and construction firm offering custom design/build services for new homes, renovations, additions, and commercial projects, with noted expertise in completing residential projects at challenging waterfront properties.

Our services include pre-design and pre-construction services, such as real estate development consulting, feasibility reports/comparisons, regulatory research, construction estimating, and cost engineering, as well as full architectural services and complete construction services, from permit to closeout.

We love what we do, and it shows in our work.
